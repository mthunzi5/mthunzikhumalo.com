About Global-Future

Building Tomorrow's Solutions Today

Global-Future is a dynamic tech company founded by Mthunzi Khumalo in Durban, South Africa. We specialize in delivering innovative software solutions for businesses, developing cutting-edge technology for education, and empowering the next generation of tech leaders.

Our flagship product, the Global Future LMS, is the continent's most comprehensive school learning management system, transforming education for thousands of learners and educators across Africa.

We are passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems, drive business growth, and create opportunities for youth development across the continent.
